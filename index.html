Mass Media

Mass Media is the use of broadcasting or communication to spread thoughts, opinions, ideas, business, or information

The Printing Press

1439

Everything from books, newspapers, and pamphlets were made by the printing press. Anything that could be reproduced using ink was done starting from the 15th century up until the 19th century.

The printing press is a form of mass media because is allowed people to spread ideas faster and to a larger population.

Photograph

1816

The first photograph was produced by Nicéphore Niépce by using a camera that he made and paper that darkened when exposed to light.

Photography is a form of mass media because it allowed people to capture moments in time and show many important events such as elections or even terrible conditions in war.

Motion Picutre

1895

The first motion picture movie was created by the Skladanowsky brothers who created a short film of a horse running.

Motion picture is a form of mass media because the start of this allowed people to make influential films that could reach out to many people.

The Ice Cream Scoop

1897

Alfred L. Cralle was a genius african american business man who created the first ever ice cream scoop.

The ice cream scoop is a mass media because it spreads happiness with each scoop of any ice cream it collects, allowing for more inventions to be made. Without this, we wouldnt have phones.

Radio Broadcasts

1920

The first ever radio broadcast was on 1920. The release of the broadcast was chosen on that day because it was election day and all the people who had radios would be able to hear the results of the election on radio instead of on the newspaper the following day.

Radio broadcasts are a form of mass media because throughout time, radio has become more diverse and have become more open to channels that provide music, political debates, or just podcasts.

Computer

1936

The first programmable computer was created by Konrad Zuse. It is considered the first functional modern computer.

Computers are a form of mass media because as they became more advanced, we were able to do more things, with the release of the internet in 1983, such as send emails, publish videos, or make websites.

Fahrenheit 451

1950

Fahrenheit 451 isnt a mass media, although, throughout the book it speaks about many inventions that we now use in the current world, yet it was published in 1950. It talks about wall wide televisions or even airpods.

Mobile Phones

1973

The first mobile phone was made by the company Motorola. They made the first handheld telephones for the world.

Mobile phones are a form of mass media because with the introduction of different apps such as twitter, facebook, or snapchat, people are able to spread their ideas to the world.
